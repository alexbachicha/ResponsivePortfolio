About Me

Hello! My name is Alex Bachicha, Alex being short for Alexandra. I was born May 20, 1993 which makes me 27 years old and a Taurus. I live in Sacramento, California with my partner and 3 other roommates. We have a pretty decently sized yard with a garden, 2 chickens, 3 ducks and 3 cats. I graduated from Sacramento State University in 2017 with my Bachelor's in Biology, emphasizing in ecology, conservation and evolution. Currently, I'm working at UC Davis's Veterinary Medical Teaching Hospital.

I've been working in the veterinary field for the past couple years and while I've enjoyed it, it's hard on my body and the hours are exhausting. I very much enjoy my free time and having more control of my life in a business sense, which prompted me to finally jump the gun and start my search for something new, particularly, in web development. It was a plus that I've always been interested in technology and video games, so I figured why not try to find something that I enjoy doing as a hobby, in addition to work. I had randomly stumbled upon information about this bootcamp. One thing led to another, and now here I am. The world of web development is entirely new to me, but so far I have enjoyed every minute of it. One thing I've already learned? Frustration is the key to success.
